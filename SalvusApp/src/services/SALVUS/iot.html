IoT Devices & Wearables (Overview)

SALVUS integrates optional IoT devices in high-risk zones. Below are typical devices and features (demo description).

  • Smart Band / Tag: Wearable with BLE + GPS that transmits periodic location & health telemetry. SOS push for immediate dispatch.
  • Environmental Beacons: Low-power radio beacons for indoor cave/forest monitoring and geofencing.
  • Health Sensors: Heart rate & fall detection to flag anomalies to the safety dashboard.
  • Edge Gateway: Local LoRa/4G gateways forward device signals securely to the cloud.

Privacy: all device data should be encrypted, opt-in, and retained only as required by law.
