About the SALVUS Platform

SALVUS is a tourism-first platform focused on promoting Northeast India while ensuring visitor safety using modern tech (digital IDs, maps, IoT, and emergency dispatch). This demo shows a prototype user experience; production requires secure APIs, official data sources and legal compliance.

Privacy & Security (Demo)

Data in this demo is stored in your browser only. For production: end-to-end encryption, consented KYC (Aadhaar/passport), data minimization, and blockchain anchoring for immutability are recommended.
